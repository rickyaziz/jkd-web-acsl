## Cara melakakukan ping

{{< alert context="info" text="<strong>Perhatian: Colok kabel jaringan patch panel 2 dengan sesuai anka anda colok pada patch panel 1</strong>"/>}}

1.  Siapkan Kabel Jaringan
    <center>
    <img src="/images/babTwo/bt-21.jpg" alt="Gambar B.1 - Jenis Kabel Jaringan" class="img-fluid mb-3 responsive-img-small">
    </center>

2.  Hubungkan kabel jaringan kabel komputer dengan patch panel 1
    <center>
    <img src="/images/babTwo/bt-22.jpg" alt="Gambar B.1 - Jenis Kabel Jaringan" class="img-fluid mb-3 responsive-img-small">
    </center>

3.  Hubungkan kabel crimping anda ke patch panel 2
    <center>
    <img src="/images/babTwo/bt-24.jpg" alt="Gambar B.1 - Jenis Kabel Jaringan" class="img-fluid mb-3 responsive-img-small">
    </center>

4.  Lalu terakhir hubungkan kabel crimping anda ke swich cisco
    <center>
    <img src="/images/babTwo/bt-25.jpg" alt="Gambar B.1 - Jenis Kabel Jaringan" class="img-fluid mb-3 responsive-img-small">
    </center>

5.  Buka control panel pada windows anda
    <center>
    <img src="/images/babTwo/bt-26.png" alt="Gambar B.1 - Jenis Kabel Jaringan" class="img-fluid mb-3">
    </center>

6.  Click Network and Internet
    <center>
    <img src="/images/babTwo/bt-27.png" alt="Gambar B.1 - Jenis Kabel Jaringan" class="img-fluid mb-3">
    </center>

7.  Click Network and Sharing Center
    <center>
    <img src="/images/babTwo/bt-28.png" alt="Gambar B.1 - Jenis Kabel Jaringan" class="img-fluid mb-3">
    </center>

8.  Click Change Adapter Settings
    <center>
    <img src="/images/babTwo/bt-29.png" alt="Gambar B.1 - Jenis Kabel Jaringan" class="img-fluid mb-3">
    </center>

9.  Click 2 kali pada Ethernet
    <center>
    <img src="/images/babTwo/bt-30.png" alt="Gambar B.1 - Jenis Kabel Jaringan" class="img-fluid mb-3">
    </center>

10. Click Properties
    <center>
    <img src="/images/babTwo/bt-31.png" alt="Gambar B.1 - Jenis Kabel Jaringan" class="img-fluid mb-3">
    </center>

11. Click 2 kali pada Internet Protocol Version 4 (TCP/IPv4)
    <center>
    <img src="/images/babTwo/bt-32.png" alt="Gambar B.1 - Jenis Kabel Jaringan" class="img-fluid mb-3">
    </center>

12. Isi ip address di kolom ip address dan subnet mask, lalu click oke
    <center>
    <img src="/images/babTwo/bt-33.png" alt="Gambar B.1 - Jenis Kabel Jaringan" class="img-fluid mb-3">
    </center>
13. buka cmd
    <center>
    <img src="/images/babTwo/bt-34.png" alt="Gambar B.1 - Jenis Kabel Jaringan" class="img-fluid mb-3">
    </center>

14. ketik ping ke jaringan anda
    <center>
    <img src="/images/babTwo/bt-35.png" alt="Gambar B.1 - Jenis Kabel Jaringan" class="img-fluid mb-3">
    </center>

15. jika output ping anda muncul maka anda berhasil melakukan ping
    <center>
    <img src="/images/babTwo/bt-36.png" alt="Gambar B.1 - Jenis Kabel Jaringan" class="img-fluid mb-3">
    </center>
